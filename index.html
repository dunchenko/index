Difference: Paralegal vs. Lawyer?
Licensed paralegals in Ontario are regulated by the Law Society of Ontario and hold the authority to represent clients only in defined courts and tribunals. Paralegals represent clients independently in Ontario Court of Justice for provincial offences and administrative tribunals. This specialized focus delivers lower costs and expert representation for matters within our jurisdiction. You receive qualified advocacy with clear scope of authority and transparent limitations.
