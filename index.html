Can I Afford This on a Tight Budget?

Absolutely. Unbundled legal services, often called limited scope retainers, let you pay only for specific tasks rather than full representation. This approach can reduce costs by 40-60%. You handle portions of your matter independently while receiving professional assistance only where needed. To explore this option, mention it in your inquiry form, by phone, or during your consultation.

Referral discounts are available for clients referred by existing customers.

Pro bono representation or reduced-fee arrangements may be offered to qualifying individuals, including refugees, displaced persons, those affected by war, and those facing financial hardship, on a case-by-case basis.

Are you a lawyer?
A licensed paralegal in Ontario means holding authority to act in defined legal matters without being a lawyer. Providing qualified representation at lower cost, offering practical alternatives to lawyers, and delivering accessible advocacy are the hallmarks of paralegal work. In many everyday disputes, paralegals function as the primary representative, ensuring efficiency, affordability, and professional service.
